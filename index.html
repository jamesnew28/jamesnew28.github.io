Welcome to Avondale Lawn Tennis Club, a great place for tennis players of all levels whether that be beginner, intermediate or advanced; there is something here for everyone as we have great coaches that work at all levels!

We have coaching sessions for all ages which are open to everyone whether you are a member or not, we have expert teaching which you will benefit greatly from and you will become a far greater tennis player than you every even dreamed of being!

Six artificial grass courts for all weather

Clubhouse with table tennis, kitchen,
changing rooms and showers and much more

Coaching at all levels - including
mini tennis for young children
